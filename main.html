The Artist

About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate ceramicist based in Budapest, Hungary. I've been working with clay for over eight years, finding beauty in the organic imperfections that make every piece truly one-of-a-kind.

My work is inspired by nature — the earthy textures of forest floors, the smooth curves of river stones. Every bowl, vase, and mug passes through my hands dozens of times before it's ready to find its home.

I work from my small studio, where everything is wheel-thrown, hand-glazed, and kiln-fired.
